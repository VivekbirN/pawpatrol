About PawPatrol

Our Mission

At PawPatrol, we are dedicated to improving the lives of street animals through rescue, rehabilitation, and rehoming efforts. Our mission is to create a world where every animal has a safe and loving home.

We work tirelessly to provide medical care, food, and shelter to animals in need, while also educating the community about responsible pet ownership and the importance of animal welfare.

Meet Our Team

Vivekbir Narula

Founder & Director

I’m Vivekbir, a 4th-semester Computer Science student and a passionate animal lover. While I haven’t been actively involved in animal rescue yet, I’m deeply committed to making a difference in the lives of street dogs and cats.

With this goal in mind, I created PawPatrol—a project I hope will grow into a meaningful platform for supporting stray animals through technology, community engagement, and compassionate action. I believe every animal deserves kindness, respect, and the chance to live a safe and happy life, and I’m excited to begin contributing to this cause.
