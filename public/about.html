About Us

Vivekbir Narula

Welcome to PawPatrol, where compassion meets action. I'm Vivekbir Narula, the founder of this initiative dedicated to improving the lives of street dogs and cats in our community.

With over 5 years of experience in animal welfare, I've witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by stray animals and the impact that dedicated care can have on their lives. My journey began when I rescued my first stray puppy, and since then, I've been committed to creating sustainable solutions for animal welfare.

At PawPatrol, we believe that every animal deserves love, care, and respect. Our team works tirelessly to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome street animals while also focusing on community education and preventive measures.

Our Mission

To create a compassionate community where no street animal suffers from neglect, hunger, or abuse. We strive to implement sustainable solutions through rescue operations, feeding programs, medical care, adoption services, and community education.

Join us in our mission to make a difference in the lives of these voiceless creatures. Together, we can create a world where every paw has a safe path to follow.
