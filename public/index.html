About Our Mission

Our Street Dogs and Cats Management System helps NGOs, municipalities, and animal lovers to effectively track, care for, and manage stray animals. We provide tools for animal registration, health tracking, feeding schedules, adoption management, lost & found reporting, and rescue coordination.

Join us in making a difference in the lives of street animals!
